### Project Overview
Sentinel AI represents our foray into blending traditional security research with cutting-edge machine learning. The goal is to move away from signature-based detection towards behavioral pattern recognition.

### Innovations
- **Transformer-based Analysis**: Applying NLP concepts to understand binary "language" and identify malicious syntax.
- **Low-latency Processing**: Optimized for edge deployment to scan high-throughput network traffic without significant overhead.

### Current Progress
We have successfully integrated the model into our core monitoring stack, achieving a 94% true-positive rate in controlled lab environments.
