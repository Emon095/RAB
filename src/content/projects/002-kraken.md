### Project Overview
With the advent of practical quantum computing on the horizon, current encryption standards face an existential threat. Project Kraken is our effort to implement and test lattice-based cryptographic schemes.

### Research Goals
- **Standardization**: Aligning with NIST guidelines while pushing the boundaries of performance.
- **Hardware Acceleration**: Designing FPGA implementations to handle the high computational demands of post-quantum algorithms.

### Impact
This project ensures that sensitive data today remains secure even decades into the future.
