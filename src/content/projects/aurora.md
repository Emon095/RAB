### Project Overview
The Aurora Borealis project was designed to test the resilience of critical infrastructure against coordinated cyber-kinetic attacks. Our team developed a custom toolset to simulate various attack vectors, including lateral movement within air-gapped networks.

### Key Features
- **Custom Payload Delivery**: Developed stealthy delivery mechanisms that bypass modern EDR solutions.
- **Protocol Analysis**: Deep dive into proprietary industrial protocols to identify undocumented vulnerabilities.
- **Reporting & Remediation**: Detailed post-operation analysis including step-by-step reproduction of all findings.

### Technical Stack
We utilized a combination of custom C++ implants, Python-based orchestration frameworks, and specialized hardware for radio-frequency interception.
